# Epic: Streamline Team Communication

This is just a sample tiki - feel free to remove!

## Vision

Transform how our teams communicate to reduce noise, improve clarity, and ensure important information reaches the right people at the right time.

## Success Criteria

| Metric | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Average email volume | 85/day | 40/day |
| Response time | 4 hours | 1 hour |
| Meeting duration | 60 min | 30 min |
| Info findability | 6/10 | 9/10 |

## Key Initiatives

### 1. Communication Guidelines
- [x] Audit current communication channels
- [x] Define channel purposes
- [ ] Create usage guidelines
- [ ] Train teams on best practices

### 2. Tool Optimization
- [ ] Consolidate messaging platforms
- [ ] Set up automated notifications
- [ ] Implement topic-based channels
- [ ] Configure priority routing

### 3. Meeting Efficiency
- [ ] Establish meeting templates
- [ ] Implement async standup option
- [ ] Create decision-making frameworks

## Dependencies

- **Technical**: Access to communication platform APIs
- **Organizational**: Buy-in from all department leads
- **Resources**: 2 weeks of dedicated team time

## Timeline

**Phase 1** (Weeks 1-2): Guidelines and training
**Phase 2** (Weeks 3-4): Tool configuration
**Phase 3** (Weeks 5-6): Rollout and iteration
